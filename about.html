About Us

Established in 2004, Mt. Everest 4th Nails & Salon is a haven of relaxation that promotes comfort, beauty, well-being, and health. Our priorities are client-focused services, high quality products, and above all else, grade-A sanitation standards.
Our expert and friendly nail technicians are dedicated that to ensuring our customers have a pleasant and memorable experience. We provide the most personalized nail salon experience at an unbeatable price. Located only a few blocks away from the M train, keep us in mind for all your manicure and pedicure needs. We aim to provide the best manicures, pedicures, and nail enhancement services in the area because our trained staff stays on top of the latest trends and techniques.

For your optimal health and safety, all equipment are properly sanitized and are individually packaged for your protection and peace of mind.
